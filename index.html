About the Author

Ashley is a first time self published author of her children’s book Fall. She enjoys writing and engaging in creative thinking & learning.
Born and raised in Miami, Fl. Ashley uses her background and surroundings as inspiration for the content she creates.
With an education in healthcare, Ashley is currently a Registered Nurse in South Florida. Although she enjoys her profession, she has now decided to take on a dual role by now being an Author!
With one book down and many more to write, this author hopes readers will enjoy the series of books and content she creates. Stay Tuned……..
